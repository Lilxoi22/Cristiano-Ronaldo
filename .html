Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born 5 February 1985)
, better known as Cristiano Ronaldo, is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward.
He plays for Premier League club Manchester United and is the captain of the Portuguese national team.
He is considered to be one of the greatest footballers of all time, and, by some, as the greatest ever.
